SEGA Computer Link: Leading IT institute, 17 years of excellence.
SEGA Computer Link has established an impressive track record of achievements that highlight our commitment to excellence in computer science education. With a team of highly qualified experts holding MSc IT degrees and boasting 17 years of invaluable teaching experience, we have consistently garnered recognition as a leading institute endorsed by CAN. We take immense pride in being the recipient of the esteemed running shield from CAN Kaski, an honor that sets us apart as the unrivaled winner in this competitive arena. Furthermore, our institute has been certified by the Nepal Government, further validating our dedication to providing top-notch education. Our active involvement in a multitude of programs organized by CAN Kaski showcases our unwavering passion for continuous growth and professional development. Notably, our remarkable achievement in the typing competition held by CAN Kaski in 2017 exemplifies the exceptional skills and talents our students possess. Additionally, we have consistently secured first positions in the quiz competitions held by CAN Kaski from 2015 to 2019, a testament to our commitment to fostering a comprehensive understanding of the subject matter. At SEGA Computer Link, we prioritize an inclusive learning experience, conducting chapter-wise exams and leveraging cutting-edge technology such as projectors to enhance theoretical teachings. Above all, our institution has created a nurturing and friendly environment that has fostered a peaceful and conducive atmosphere for students over the past 17 years.
